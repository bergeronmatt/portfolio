About

Hi, I'm Matt. I'm a UI/UX Designer living in Columbus, OH. If you take a loot at my resume below, you'll see that I'm not your average visual designer. I originally went to University and received an undergraduate degree in Applied Exercise Science and a graduate degree in Sports Performance. I wanted to be a professional Strength Coach at the collegiate and professional level, and help as many people as possible through my online business. At one point I was a professional athlete with several sponsors, and even working for one of them as a wholesale manager. Unfortunatly, that business went bankrupt, and I suffered a pretty nasty injury that took me out of competing for a while.

I found out quickly that in the world of Athletics and Fitness, most people don't care about how good of a coach you are, or how well educated you may be, they really only care about how popular you are. I lost my job, my sponsorships, and I'm back at square-one so to speak career wise, but compared to what I lost, I gained 10 fold more. I had to teach myself about marketing online, both with websites and social media. I taught myself HTML/CSS, visual design, and I'm even in training now to become a Full Stack Web Developer so I can create a project front to back. I've learned from the companies I worked with not only on how to be successful, but on what to avoid so as to have a powerful reputation, and avoid critical failures.

I am relatively new at UI/UX design, but I already have the skill not only to design projects, but bring them to life. I have been taught through adversity, and found the skills on my own to begin to succeed not just in one field, but another. I have more than tenacity, I have dedication.
