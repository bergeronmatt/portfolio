Intro

I'm Matt, I'm a Web Designer and a soon to be Full Stack Web Developer. I'm based in Columbus, OH, and I help people make their websites and app ideas into a reality.

I'm a former Strength and Conditioning coach, so I understand hard work and dedication. Instead of using that drive to help build up athletes and bodies I now use it to design and build websites to bring the ideas and dreams of others to life.

Work

Ladybeef, Inc

Web Design, Web Development, Web Management

Ladybeef, Inc is an online fitness and diet consultation service. The client asked for a redesign of the site based off of their self-made site on a CMS platform. The project resulted in a complete redesign based off of contemporaries in the field as well personal preferences from the client. I know manage this site, including optimizing and updating SEO throughout the year, editing and uploading content, with plans of building web applications for their coaching services.

Better Professor

Web Design, Web Development

Better Professor is a hypothetical application for University professors to better manage graduate students they are mentoring. The app allows users, students and professors alike, to communicate and set reminders with each other and themselves for important projects, including research and letters of recommendations. I was responsible for designing the marketing site and web application as well as building the marketing site itself.

About

Hi, I'm Matt, and I'm not your usual Web Designer. I actually went to University, where I received two degrees, and Bachelor's in Applied Exercise Science and a Master's in Sports and Human Performance. Following my graduation, I worked in the private sector as a strength and conditioning, olympic weightlifting, CrossFit, and diet coach. I was even on a professional weightlifting team for a while, and worked my way up the ranks in the sport.

Through seven+ years in a highly competitive industry, I had to teach myself about marketing and advertising for my business, as well as the strength and conditioning company I was employed by while competing professionally. The company eventually shut down, and I continued to work in the industry as a coach, but the level of competition in the industry and the low amount of compensation drove me to learn more skills to promote myself and my business>

I bought some old text books from an electronics store and taught myself the basics to html/css/js and UI/UX. I assissted friends and contemporaries with designing their websites, apps, and even ebooks and digital goods to gain experience. It wasn't until recently, after pursuing a Full Stack Web Development training program, that I began to do freelance work in Web Design and Development. I may be new to Web Design and Web Development, but with my varied background, education, and athletic tenacity, I can tackle any project with a unique skill set.
